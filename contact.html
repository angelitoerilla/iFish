About us

i-Fish (Information on Fishes), a website to guide hobbyist who wanted to set-up an aquarium. It provides choices and details for both freshwater fishes and plants.
The author of the site showed interest on fish care and tank maintenance when his older sister bought a set-up tank. When the website owner got his own kids, he set-up his own aquarium to entertain toddlers at home.
